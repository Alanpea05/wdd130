Content

Home page

Taná was creating because we think that we have to change the way men fashion works, we can see fashion in women change a lot every year, that’s how this idea borned, we visited a lot of renters locals of ties and suits, and we realize they have the same style, they don’t have any novelty, even we found the same ties to rent and sell, Taná helps Mexican traditions to grow creating new designs of men fashion based on traditions and culture.

The principals designs are from a little Town in Larrainzar, Chiapas, where the idea came to us, one of the main activities there is embroider and create crafts to sell, but they don’t have a lot of market, we had the opportunity to know them and we want to help them and also make known the beauty of their culture and tradition, We could find a lot of colors and different designs, we want you to feel free to navigate and feel enthusiasm every time you see the different products we would like to offer, also combine your style with this new way to look. The idea of this web page is that the people who enter fall in love with the designs and the Mexican culture that will be printed on each of the ties and some designs of different products, we believe that it would be very important to promote more talent and national products to help the country and also so that everyone can see that the local product is equal to or better than the conventional one, we will try to have the best possible variety so that customers have many options for any type of outfit

The difference that we will have with the others is the originality and innovation in our designs, the idea of a product made entirely by hand at home can be a very important factor to make it known that each of the products is made with a special and specific dedication, we will also try to improve customer service and the packaging and distribution experience, we believe that you should fall in love from the first sight, that is why we seek to provide the best in packaging and unpacking.

The variety of our products will be mainly focused on the sale of ties, bows and accessories for men, however we know that the opportunities can be very diverse and we want to be able to cover as many people as possible, we hope that through the demand and innovation, we can expand our designs and catalog to a wider variety of products for different markets and ages, so that everyone has the opportunity to enjoy wearing a piece of Mexican culture.

Contact with customers is very important to know about the quality of our products and how we can improve as a company, we hope that our main market will be Mexicans who are proud of the ancient cultures of our country, so they are also participants in this idea, we want to have contact with others through any of our social networks.

We believe that sharing about us can also be a good factor to create a link with others, being transparent is always going to be a good idea to connect with others and that they have enough confidence to order their products with us, we hope to have dynamics also to meet others and create a good community with which to be able to interact in some moments of the company's history, in addition our information will be important so that the location of the store is known so that those who may have the opportunity can come to see and know the products in a more direct way.
